Lasagna

a classic baked pasta dish made with wide, flat noodles layered with fillings like ragù, béchamel, and cheeses

Ingredients

  • 2 Olive Oil
  • 750g Lean Mince Beef
  • 90g prosciutto
  • 800g passata sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock
  • nutmeg
  • 300g lasagna sheets
  • white sauce
  • 125g ball mozzerella

Steps

  1. Boil pasta: In a large pot of salted water boil lasagna noodles per the recipe below.
  2. Prepare meat sauce: Cook sausage and beef with onion and garlic. Drain well, add the pasta sauce & simmer it for a few minutes to thicken.
  3. Combine cheese mixture: Stir the cheese mixture together in a bowl.
  4. Layer & bake: Layer the meat sauce and cheese mixture with lasagna noodles and bake until the top of the lasagna is golden brown.
  5. Once removed from the oven, let the lasagna sit/rest for at least 15 minutes (up to 45 minutes is fine, it will retain the heat well). This will allow it to thicken up a bit and help it keep its shape when cut. Resting is not required when reheating.
